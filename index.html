Wrapped Litecoin, One Cryptocurrency, 4 Ethereum Sidechains

wLTC Brings the power and usability of an ERC20 to Litecoin, with gas fees lower than you can imagine, and block speeds that will simply blow your mind.

Right,  Elaborate

Wrapped Litecoin is the next step in the inclusion of Ethereum side-chains which allows much faster and ultra-low cost transactions/smart contract interactions.

Additionally, Wrapped Litecoin will enable anyone to invest in projects, add liquidity, stake, hold and swap Litecoin on multiple Ethereum side-chains. These include the Matic and Expanse side-chains, both of which allow easy smart contract creation and integration, as-well as using Web3 for transactions and interactions.

Low Fees

Ethereum Side-Chains allow for fees that hover around 1 1000th of a penny, which in turn means there are virtually free.

Extra Fast Block Speeds

All of the Side-Chains wLTC operates on have a sub 1 minute average block time, with the fastest being the Matic network, which aims for a consistent and impressive 2 second block time.

Backed 1 : 1 By Litecoin at all times

Thats right, no schemes here! Wrapped Litecoin is backed 1:1 by Litecoin, which means if you have 1 wLTC, you can redeem it for 1 LTC at any given time(and vice versa of course!).

Frequently Asked Questions

When will wLTC be launched officially?

Wrapped Litecoin is aiming for a middle of march 2021 release date. Join our discord server, follow our twitter and join our subreddit at the bottom of this page for updates and much more project information!

Is there some kind of beta program?

If you take a look at our discord server, you will find a wLTC beta channel, which has a step by step guide on how to join our beta program to get a feel for how wLTC will function! Not only will this allow you to familiarize yourself with the dynamics of an ERC20, but you will also receive the V.I.P. role on our discord server permanently for extra access and invite only events!

Where can I get Wrapped Litecoin?

Wrapped Litecoin will be available on many exchanges, but our primary minting operator and LTC custodian will be announced soon, and you may wrap your LTC into wLTC and vice versa with ease in minutes for absolutely no cost! Additionally, you will be able to use ChainBridge in order to move wLTC to and from the 4 side-chains we operate on, a real multi-chain currency is on the rise!

How many wLTC will exist? 

The maximum amount of wLTC that could ever exist is 84 Million, coincidentally (Not really) it is exactly the same amount of maximum LTC to ever exist. Same applies to the decimal count of wLTC, with 8 decimals.

How are the fees so low?

Because we operate on Ethereum side-chains, we are able to provide fees that are much lower than traditional LTC fees, even though they are already low compared to Bitcoin or Ethereum which have fees currently ranging from 10-40$ USD and 30-80$ USD per transaction respectively. These side-chains use state of the art and updated Proof of stake systems as-well as better set up mining programs to ensure we can continue allowing you to move LTC at a fraction of the cost, usually ending up costing between 1 100th to 1 1000th of a cent per transaction.

Are side-chains as safe and useable as the Ethereum Mainnet?

Ethereum side-chains are completely decentralized just like the Main-Net, but offer much needed aid to the ecosystem such as ultra low fees and insane block times which puts the meaning of easy into crypto. Not only that, side-chains allow for much easier liquidity addition and management, as-well as making staking accessible to anyone with any amount of cryptocurrency, wether its 5 dollars or 5 Million dollars.
